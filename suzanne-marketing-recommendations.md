## Website Strategy & Messaging

### Aesthetics
- **Problem:** The site's single-color design is difficult to scan
- **Action:** Add white space and distinct sections with different background colors to improve readability
- **Action:** Remove the white background from the logo in the navigation bar to appear more professional
- **Action:** Make the nav bar white, like most other websites

### Messaging
- **Problem:** The site's purpose is unclear to new visitors
- **Action:** Change the main headline from "Welcome to the Omega Elite Launchpad" to "Your central hub for all things peptides"
- **Action:** Move the explainer video to the homepage to build trust and context before presenting offers

### User Journey
- **Problem:** The site presents offers and forms too early
- **Action:** Place forms and pricing after the value proposition is established
- **Action:** Fix the broken, unbranded "Quick Sign Up" page, which appears suspicious

### Trust
- **Problem:** The site's testimonials undermine credibility
- **Action:** Replace all anonymous testimonials with full names and photos
- **Quote from Suzanne:** "If that's a true testimonial, why isn't their full name there? Why isn't their photo there? Why isn't there more? Anybody could have just made this up."

### Asset Monetization
- **Problem:** The "Trusted Partners" list gives away valuable research for free
- **Action:** Gate this list behind a paid program
- **Action:** Reframe the section to highlight the value of this research and how paid members get access to it

## Business Model & Scalability

### Problem
- Jason's current model is unsustainable, causing burnout
- The "Peptide Coach" app, intended for automation, still requires manual protocol tweaks

### Solution
- Adopt a 90/10 model (90% automated, 10% high-ticket) to scale
- **Automated:** Keep the low-cost practitioner training and Omega Elite community
- **High-Ticket:** Raise the price of the intensive 1-on-1 program (currently $14-18k) to $25-30k

### Target Audience Decision Required
- **Consumers:** A large, underserved market, especially women, who need guidance beyond weight loss
- **Practitioners:** Continue with the automated training, but raise the price of the intensive 1-on-1 program

## Repackage Content
- The $69/mo Omega Elite community's high-value courses should be repackaged as a standalone, higher-priced product to better capture their worth

## Peptide Coach App Feedback
- **Goal:** Drive engagement in the community forum, not private coach chat
- **Suggestion:** Incentivize community participation with tangible rewards instead of leaderboards
- **Examples:** 
  - Discount on the next peptide order (e.g., 15% → 20%)
  - Free, low-cost product shipped to high contributors
