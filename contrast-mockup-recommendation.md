# Landing Page Contrast Redesign Recommendation

## The Problem: Not Enough Contrast

Your marketing expert Suzanne is right. Looking at hims.com, forhers.com, and joinmidi.com, they all use **full-width colored sections** to create visual rhythm and contrast—not just accent colors on white backgrounds.

Currently, your landing page is predominantly white/cream with orange used only as small accents. This makes everything blend together and lacks the visual "punch" that draws the eye.

## The Solution: Strategic Color Blocking with Orange/Navy

Instead of sprinkling orange accents on white, we should use **full-width navy and orange sections** alternating with white sections. This creates the same visual rhythm as the competitor sites while staying true to your brand.
