# Contrast Analysis: hims, forhers, joinmidi

## Key Contrast Techniques Observed

### 1. HIMS.COM - Bold Color Blocking
- **Hero Section**: White background with product cards
- **Feature Sections**: Full-width GOLD/BROWN colored sections (not just accents)
- **Alternating Pattern**: White → Gold → Sage Green → White
- **Text on Color**: White text on colored backgrounds for maximum contrast
- **CTA Buttons**: White buttons on colored backgrounds, colored buttons on white

### 2. FORHERS.COM - Soft Pastels with Bold Accents
- **Hero**: White with soft pink/coral accents
- **Feature Sections**: Full-width PINK/CORAL sections
- **Alternating Pattern**: White → Pink → White → Coral
- **Photography**: High-contrast lifestyle images

### 3. JOINMIDI.COM - Warm Earth Tones
- **Hero**: White/cream background
- **Feature Sections**: Full-width TERRACOTTA/RUST colored sections
- **Trust Section**: Soft pink background
- **Pattern**: White → Terracotta → White → Pink

## Common Patterns (What Creates the Contrast)

1. **Full-Width Color Blocks** - Not just accent colors, but ENTIRE SECTIONS with colored backgrounds
2. **White Text on Dark/Colored Backgrounds** - Creates strong readability
3. **Alternating Rhythm** - White section → Colored section → White section
4. **Bold CTAs** - Contrasting button colors that pop against backgrounds
5. **Strategic Photography** - High-quality images with good contrast

## Recommendations for Omega Longevity (Orange/Navy Scheme)

### Current Issue
- Too much white/cream throughout
- Orange only used as small accents
- Navy barely visible
- Everything blends together

### Proposed Contrast Strategy

#### Section 1: Hero (Keep White)
- White background
- Orange gradient headline (keep current)
- Navy accent text for tagline

#### Section 2: What's Inside (NAVY BACKGROUND)
- Full-width navy (#1e3a5f) background
- White text and icons
- Orange accent badges
- Creates strong contrast after white hero

#### Section 3: Why Omega Longevity (White)
- Return to white background
- Navy text
- Orange checkmarks/icons

#### Section 4: How It Works (ORANGE/AMBER BACKGROUND)
- Full-width warm orange/amber (#f59e0b or #ea580c) background
- Navy or white text
- White step numbers

#### Section 5: Ecosystem Platforms (White)
- White background
- Navy card borders
- Orange CTA buttons

#### Section 6: Testimonials (NAVY BACKGROUND)
- Full-width navy background
- White text
- Orange quote marks and result badges

#### Section 7: Final CTA (White with Orange Button)
- White background
- Large orange CTA button
- Navy supporting text

### Color Palette for Contrast
- **Primary Navy**: #1e3a5f (deep, professional)
- **Secondary Navy**: #0f172a (darker for headers)
- **Primary Orange**: #f59e0b (warm amber)
- **Accent Orange**: #ea580c (deeper orange for CTAs)
- **White**: #ffffff
- **Off-White**: #fefce8 (warm cream for subtle sections)

### Visual Rhythm
```
WHITE (Hero) → NAVY (What's Inside) → WHITE (Trust) → ORANGE (How It Works) → WHITE (Platforms) → NAVY (Testimonials) → WHITE (CTA)
```

This creates a strong visual rhythm like hims/forhers/midi while using your brand colors.
