# Landing Page Redesign: Mockup & Recommendations

## Executive Summary

Based on analysis of **hims.com**, **forhers.com**, and **joinmidi.com**, the marketing expert's feedback is validated: leading health/wellness sites use predominantly **white backgrounds** with strategic accent colors to create a premium, trustworthy, and easy-to-focus experience.

---

## Current vs. Recommended Design

| Element | Current | Recommended |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Background** | Dark slate (navy/charcoal) | White with soft accent sections |
| **Navigation** | Dark with orange CTA | White with clean, minimal design |
| **Hero** | Dark gradient | White/cream with orange gradient text |
| **Cards** | Dark slate with borders | White cards with subtle shadows |
| **White Space** | Minimal | Generous (60-70% of page) |
| **CTA Placement** | Top of page | After value proposition |

## Specific Changes Requested

**After:** Clean white background with:

### 2. Hero Section Changes
**Headline Change:**
- **Before:** "Welcome to the Omega Elite Launchpad"

**Tagline (keep as-is):**

**Before:** "Create Account in 10 Seconds" button immediately in hero

## Color Palette Recommendation

| Use | Current | Recommended |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| **Primary Background** | slate-900 | white (#FFFFFF) |
| **Secondary Background** | slate-800 | cream/off-white (#FAFAFA) |
| **Accent Sections** | slate-800/50 | soft amber (#FFF8F0) |
| **Primary Text** | white | dark gray (#1F2937) |
| **Secondary Text** | slate-400 | medium gray (#6B7280) |
| **Accent Color** | amber-400/orange-500 | Keep as-is (gradient) |
| **CTA Buttons** | amber-to-orange gradient | Keep as-is |
| **Cards** | slate-800/50 | white with shadow |

---

## Typography Recommendations

Based on competitor analysis:
- **Headlines:** Keep bold, but use dark text on white background
- **Body:** Keep current sizing, use gray on white for better readability
- **Accent text:** Keep orange/amber gradient for emphasis
